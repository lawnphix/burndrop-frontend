How it works
01 · Upload
Client-side encryption
Your file is encrypted with AES-256-GCM in your browser before it leaves your device. We never see the unencrypted content.
02 · Share
One-time link
You get a unique link containing the decryption key. Only someone with that exact link can decrypt and download the file.
03 · Burn
Self-destructs on download
The moment the recipient downloads the file it is permanently deleted from our servers. Any further attempts return a 404.
